Our Story

We met at work around 2019 through our best pals who are now our Best Man and Maid of Honor. We started as friends for quite some time as we were both at a place where we weren’t looking for a relationship. Until later on our casual hangouts became dating (wooing phase).

On August 31, 2020, on the way home after our weekend long ride vacation, we stopped by Barili Bay Viewpoint to admire the view. It was there that we finally and officially became a couple. What happened next were a lot more dates, rides, campings, and just travels in general. We shared and have been sharing each other’s life’s ups and downs together — hugs and kisses included!

Then on the night of October 15, 2021, she walked to a path of petals and lit candles leading to more petals shaped in a heart with lit candles surrounding it, and a ballad OPM song playing in the background. There she met him wearing a suit and tie, kneeling down on one knee, holding out an emerald ring. With a trembling voice out of emotion, he asked for her hand in marriage. She said YES.
On Christmas that same year he flew with her to Zamboanga City for the first time to formally meet her parents, and share the news.

Here we are in 2024. Now a few days before the big day. We can't wait to celebrate with you at our wedding ceremony and reception.

Our Story

We first crossed paths at work back in 2019, introduced by our closest friends who now stand by our side as our Best Man and Maid of Honor. Our connection began as a friendship, both content in a place where relationships weren't our priority. But over time, our casual hangouts blossomed into something deeper.

It was on August 31, 2020, after a weekend adventure, that we found ourselves at Barili Bay Viewpoint. As we gazed at the breathtaking view together, we took the leap into officially becoming a couple. From that moment on, our days were filled with dates, road trips, camping under the stars, and exploring the world hand in hand. We've shared every joy and challenge, embracing each other with hugs and kisses along the way.

Then, on the magical night of October 15, 2021, he led her along a path adorned with petals and softly glowing candles, forming a heart shape around them. A sweet OPM ballad played in the background as he knelt before her on one knee in formal attire, heart racing with emotion. With a trembling voice, he asked for her hand in marriage, and without hesitation, she said YES.
That Christmas, he flew with her to Zamboanga City for the first time, a journey filled with nerves and excitement as he met her parents and shared the wonderful news.

And now, here we are in 2024, just days away from our big day. We are overflowing with excitement to celebrate our love and commitment with you at our wedding ceremony and reception.
